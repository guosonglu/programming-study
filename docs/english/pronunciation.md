# 元音

## /ɪ/

- `is`: /ɪz/
- `it`: /ɪt/

## /iː/

- `me`: /miː/
- `please`: /pliːz/

## /ə/

- `umbrella`: /ʌmˈbrelə/
- `teacher`: /ˈtiːtʃə/

## /ɜː/

- `sir`: /sɜːr/
- `shirt`: /ʃɜːrt/

## /ɒ/

- `watch`: /wɒtʃ/
- `what`: /wɒt/

## /ɔː/

- `your`: /jɔːr/
- `tall`: /tɔːl/

## /ʊ/

- `book`:/bʊk/
- `good`:/gʊd/

## /uː/

- `food`:/fuːd/
- `too`:/tuː/

## /ʌ/

- `son`:/sʌn/
- `but`:/bʌt/

## /ɑː/

- `father`:/ˈfɑːðər/
- `car`:/kɑːr/

## /e/

- yes:/jes/
- bed:/bed/

## /æ/

- hand:/hænd/
- cat:/kæt/
